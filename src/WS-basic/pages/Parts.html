Parts

Overview

This year, our parts can be divided into categories of three.

Plasmids for basic constructs

This category includes the plasmids of original proteins or protein fragments, ranging from 1RBD of hACE2, 2spike protein monomer, 3protease(for spike protein cleavage), and 4red fluorescent protein

Plasmids for constructs of detection assay

This category includes the modified plasmids which are finally translated into protein complex that is used in our spike detection FRET assay. These include 1mVenus-RBD of hACE2, 2mScarlet-I-Spike protein monomer

Plasmids for constructs of spike cleavage design

This category includes the plasmid for the spike protein cleavage design, which will finally be translated into the construct of “Protease-RBD of hACE2”
