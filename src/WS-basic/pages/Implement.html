Implementation

Our target groups and how to use our project

  1. Contact lens
    We designed our contact lens in the hope of protecting people from infection through eyes. Therefore, the target group will be those who is used to wearing contact lens. Compared to people wearing glasses, people who wear contact lenses lack a physical barrier, so we want provide them with a accustomed way to resist virus.
  2. Mask
    Masks are universally used to protect us from viruses. We, of course, want to promote this product to all human beings. We expect that people can get used to wearing masks when going out.
  3. Detection box
    Customs is a crutial place when it comes to epidemic prevention. We designed this portable and costless box hoping that we can quickly make a simple test when people enter another country to reduce the risk of virus spreading.

Implement our project in the real world

We looking forward to cooperating with mask and contact lens companies which we had visited and exchanged opinions about our project when we conceived our entire haedware design. By cooperating, we can not only really understand whole situation of their market deeper but also improve our designs, making them meet the reality application more.

Safty that we considered

Contact lens

  1. We spare the central position not to conjugated with protein in order not to cause vision blur.
  2. We should make sure protein have bound hydrogel firmly in cas they fall off when blinking.

Masks

  1. We tried to avoid that our hydrogel account for too much area to influence the fluency of breathing.

Detacion box

  1. The waste after detection shuld be saved well so as to avoid making virus in it leak out.
  2. We designed to let users just exhale rather than collect their sputum by cotton swab, which makes detection process less umcomfortable.

The challenges

  1. If we want promote our products, we should have them coorespond to the law about medical equipment.
  2. How to reduce the degration of protein is also a challenge we need to consider.
  3. Whether the protease will cause effect in human body is unknown, so it’s a big challenge now, for we can’t conduct human experiment.
